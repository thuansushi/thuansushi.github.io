Our Story

Tradition Meets Innovation

Founded in 2023, Thuan Sushi has been serving authentic Japanese cuisine with a modern twist. Our master chefs have trained in Tokyo and bring decades of experience to every dish we create.

We source only the freshest, highest-quality ingredients, with our fish delivered daily from local markets. Our commitment to quality and tradition has made us a favorite among sushi connoisseurs.

At Thuan Sushi, we believe that dining is an experience. From our carefully crafted menu to our elegant ambiance, every detail is designed to transport you to the heart of Japan.
